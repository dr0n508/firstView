How can I follow my student’s stop?

Following your student’s stop is the most important part of FirstView™; it’s how FirstView™ works for you. From the Profile screen, scroll down and select “Follow A Stop.” Choose the proper state and school district, then insert the ID of the student associated with that stop and select “Done.” The stop will appear on your Profile screen under “Student Stops.”

How can I set or update distance reminders?

Distance reminders tell you how far, in miles, the bus is from your selected stop. To set up or update your distance reminders please navigate to your Profile. Once on your Profile screen, scroll down to select the stop for which you’d like to set up reminders. Select “Distance Reminders” and choose the preferred distance. You can select multiple options.

How do I set up time-based reminders?

Time-based reminders tell you how many minutes until your bus will arrive at your selected stop. To set up or update your time-based reminders navigate to your Profile. Once on your Profile screen, scroll down to select the stop for which you’d like to set up the reminders. Select “Time-Based Reminders” and choose the preferred time. You can select multiple options.
